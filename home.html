Our Story

Flavours Cooked with Love & Tradition

Mamta Bhojnalaya was born out of a simple dream — to serve honest, home-cooked Indian food to everyone who walks through our doors. From fragrant dals simmered all morning to crispy rotis rolled fresh for you, we believe good food is the truest expression of care.

We keep our menu seasonal, our spices freshly ground, and our portions generous. Whether you're a student grabbing a quick meal or a family celebrating a quiet evening, you deserve food that feels like home.
